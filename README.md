# Tulane Sports Medicine (AT)

A modern iOS scheduling and athlete management app built for Tulane Sports Medicine. This project was created to improve communication and scheduling workflows between athletic trainers and Tulane student-athletes through a cleaner, faster, and more intuitive mobile experience.

As a Division I student-athlete myself, I experienced firsthand how frustrating and outdated sports medicine scheduling systems can be. I wanted to build something that actually feels modern, efficient, and athlete-friendly while still serving the operational needs of trainers and staff.

---

## Why I Built This

Current sports medicine scheduling systems are often:

* difficult to navigate
* outdated visually
* inefficient for athletes on busy schedules
* not optimized for mobile-first workflows

This project reimagines that experience with a streamlined iOS application focused on usability, accessibility, and real-world athletic department workflows.

The app was designed with the goal of supporting 400+ Tulane student-athletes and improving the day-to-day scheduling experience for both athletes and trainers.

---
